About

The man

Born thirty years ago I’m Matt Riggott, an Englishman in exile in Glasgow, Scotland. I’ve been there since April 2009 after moving from Edinburgh, the place I’d called home for over six years. I live in The Weeg with my lovely lady, Alison. Some interesting things I’ve done are:

  • As a wee man I lived in Kuwait.
  • A couple of years ago I ran the Reykjavik marathon.
  • I learned Spanish for years, to the point I could describe myself as utterly mediocre at the language.
  • I lived in St Louis, Missouri for a couple of months.
  • I wandered around South America and Patagonia for a little while.
  • Bizarrely, I was the cover model for the 2007 Edinburgh International Film Festival.

The site
